Caprese Salad

Description

Caprese Salad! This summer salad is a delight to eat when it's hot out. Whoever was smart enough to invent this damn thing was a genius. All the elements of this creation work in harmony to create a light, delicious meal.

This is more of a guide than a recipe. Much like learning to code (i presume), this will impart the foundations upon you- but the rest is up to your creativity. A meal that can be perfected purely with vibes- yes please!

Ingredients

  • Tomato(es)
  • Mozzerella
  • Olive oil (optional)
  • Balsamic Vinegar (optional)
  • Salt n Pepper (optional)
  • Basil Leaves (optional)

Steps

  1. Slice your tomatoes as desired
  2. Slice your Mozzerella as desired
  3. Decorate and enchant with other ingredients as desired. This is your canvas!
